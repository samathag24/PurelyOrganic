What is Organic Farming?

Organic farming is an agricultural method that aims to produce food using natural substances and processes. This means avoiding synthetic chemicals such as pesticides and fertilizers. Instead, organic farming relies on crop rotation, compost, and biological pest control to maintain soil health and ensure sustainable food production.
